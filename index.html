The Artist

Utkarsh Tripathi

Fine Artist

Utkarsh Tripathi is an artist specializing in graphite, charcoal, and mixed media. His work focuses on blending traditional portraiture techniques with a modern aesthetic, seeking to capture the unique, soulful essence of his subjects.

Believing that art must capture not just a likeness but a memory, he personally guides every commission from the first conversation to the final stroke. This ensures each piece is a timeless, deeply personal work.
